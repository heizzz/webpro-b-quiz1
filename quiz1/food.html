Roti Mantau
The typical bread for breakfast for people in Balikpapan is roti mantau. Unlike other types of bread that contain chocolate or sweets, mantau bread is suitable for breakfast companions. It is a must try for you who love to have bread for breakfast.
Amplang
There is no denying that amplang is a typical food that is always missed and is the target of the main souvenirs. The majority of people like fish crackers, but what is characteristic of fish crackers from Balikpapan is the fish material made from belida fish.
Pisang Gapit
A snack that is always missed is pisang gapit from Balikpapan. Made from the basic ingredients of "pisang gepok" which are burned on charcoal until half cooked. After that, the bananas are pinched with wood and then burned again until ripe.
Abon Kepiting
Shredded meat lovers must try this typical Balikpapan souvenir. Usually, in other places, the types of shredded are shredded chicken, mutton, and beef. Well, in Balikpapan, you will be presented with a special shredded menu with the name shredded crab.
