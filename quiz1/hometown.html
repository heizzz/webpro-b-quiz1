Balikpapan
Clean, Beautiful, Safe, and Comfortable City
The Gateway to East Kalimantan
Balikpapan is the gateway to the economy of East Kalimantan because all economic activities must pass through this city. Semayang Harbor and Sultan Aji Muhammad Sulaiman Airport are two of the busiest and most important entrances.
Adipura Kencana Awardee
Adipura is an award for cities in Indonesia that have succeeded in cleaning and managing the urban environment. Adipura is organized by the State Ministry of Environment and Adipura Kencana is an award for cities that have won 5 Adipura awards in a row.
Culture
Erau Pelas Benua
The Erau Pelas Benua ceremony is a traditional celebration to commemorate the birthday of the Kutai Kartanegara Ing Martadipura Kingdom which was held in conjunction with the anniversary of Balikpapan City. Given that Balikpapan was also a part of the kingdom, the Erau Pelas Benua ceremony is held every year in Balikpapan.
Rumah Adat Lamin
Rumah Lamin is a traditional house from East Kalimantan. Rumah Lamin is the identity of the Dayak people in East Kalimantan. Rumah Lamin is about 300 meters long, 15 meters wide, and approximately 3 meters high. Rumah Lamin is also known as a house on stilts which is long from connecting.
Pakaian Adat Takwo
The Takwo dress is one of the traditional clothes used by the Kutai people in weddings. In the past, takwo clothes were only worn by aristocrats or dancers during traditional ceremonies. Now the general public can wear takwo clothes as wedding clothes. Quoted from the Balikpapan City Government website, Takwo means friendship.
